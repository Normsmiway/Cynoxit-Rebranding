  • USMAN A. JAURO

    MD/CEO

    About

    Usman Abubakar Jauro is a graduate of Information Systems with concentration on Software Applications Development from the American University of Nigeria. He also holds an MSc. in Supply Chain Management from Robert Gordon University Aberdeen, United Kingdom. Before joining Cynox, he was the Strategy and Planning Officer of Sigma Pensions Ltd, he was also the Chief Information Officer of Laminga Farms Nigeria Limited where he developed, modified, installed, tested and maintained computer software and hardware systems. Mr. Jauro also has vast experience in assisting computer personnel in evaluating computer programs design to ensure the most efficient utilization of software systems and to avoid incompatibility with other software. Usman has expertise in technical support and Application Development in .NET. As a Senator of the Student Government Association, he chaired the Committee on Information Technology.
