CYNOX IT is a privately owned Information and Communications Technology firm established in 2010. In order to effectively serve its clients, the company has evolved from CYNOX-OMEGA TECH SOLUTIONS to CYNOX IT LTD

CYNOX IT has a group of well experienced IT professionals with significant transaction experience in different spheres of endeavor in its staff complements, with backgrounds in Information Systems, Software Engineering, Telecommunications & Wireless Technologies, and an amazing zeal to succeed, all of which are brought to bear in our mode of service delivery which is specifically targeted at meeting the challenging aspirations of businesses.

At CYNOX IT we seek to integrate capacity building with specialized consulting and advisory services to create successful and sustainable projects. We believe that, successful project are measured not only by the advice provided and solutions deployed, but also by the capacity developed and left behind with clients.
